## Spicy Vegetarian Sopa Azteca (Tortilla Soup) with Ancho, Pasilla & Guajillo Chiles
**Last made:** 2025-11-30
**Rating:** 5/5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Time:** 60 minutes
**Difficulty:** Medium

**Ingredients:**
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- 4 cups tomatoes (diced)
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- 1 onion (diced)
- 4 cloves garlic (minced)
- 2-3 ancho chiles
- 1-2 pasilla chiles
- 2 guajillo chiles
- 2-3 Serrano peppers (sliced)
- 4 corn tortillas
- 1½ cups corn kernels
- 1 avocado (sliced)
- 3 tablespoons fresh cilantro
- 3 tablespoons fresh recao
- 1 lime
- salt
- pepper

**Notes:** We added black beans which gave it a bit of depth. Don’t forget to de-seed the chilis and watch the heat

**Make again:** Yes
