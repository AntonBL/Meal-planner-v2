## Coconut Curry Lentil Soup
**Cuisine:** Asian / Indian
**Type:** Soup
**Last made:** 2025-11-25
**Rating:** 5/5
**Times made:** 18

**Ingredients:**
- Red lentils (1 cup)
- Coconut milk (1 can)
- Vegetable broth (4 cups)
- Onion, garlic, ginger
- Curry powder, cumin, turmeric
- Spinach
- Lime juice

**Notes:**
Our absolute favorite! So warming and comforting. Reheats perfectly for leftovers throughout the week. Can add more vegetables (carrots, bell peppers) if we have them. Takes about 30 minutes.

## Ginger Garlic Stir-Fried Vegetables
**Cuisine:** Asian / Chinese
**Last made:** 2025-11-22
**Rating:** 5/5
**Times made:** 25

**Ingredients:**
- Mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas)
- Tofu or tempeh (cubed and pan-fried)
- Fresh ginger and garlic
- Soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar
- Served over rice or rice noodles

**Notes:**
Quick weeknight favorite! Use whatever veggies we have. The key is fresh ginger and garlic - makes all the difference. Can be ready in 20 minutes. Great for using up vegetables before they go bad.

## Mediterranean Chickpea Salad
**Cuisine:** Mediterranean
**Type:** Salad
**Last made:** 2025-11-20
**Rating:** 5/5
**Times made:** 15

**Ingredients:**
- Chickpeas (2 cans, drained)
- Cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion
- Feta cheese
- Olive oil, lemon juice
- Fresh parsley and basil
- Salt, pepper, oregano

**Notes:**
Perfect for warm days or when we don't want to cook. Very filling and fresh. Can make ahead and store in fridge for 2-3 days. Sometimes add olives or roasted red peppers.

## Sheet Pan Roasted Vegetables with Tahini
**Cuisine:** Middle Eastern
**Type:** Roasted vegetables
**Last made:** 2025-11-18
**Rating:** 5/5
**Times made:** 20

**Ingredients:**
- Mixed vegetables (cauliflower, broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
- Olive oil
- Spices (cumin, paprika, garlic powder)
- Tahini sauce (tahini, lemon, garlic, water)
- Served with quinoa or couscous

**Notes:**
So easy! Just chop, toss, roast at 425°F for 25-30 minutes. The tahini sauce makes it special. Make extra vegetables for leftovers - can use in salads or grain bowls later in the week.

## Black Bean & Sweet Potato Tacos
**Cuisine:** Mexican
**Type:** Beans/legumes
**Last made:** 2025-11-15
**Rating:** 5/5
**Times made:** 12

**Ingredients:**
- Black beans (2 cans)
- Sweet potato (cubed and roasted)
- Tortillas
- Avocado, lime, cilantro
- Cheese, salsa
- Cumin, chili powder

**Notes:**
Hearty and satisfying! Roast the sweet potatoes while warming the beans. Top with avocado, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime. Can meal prep the sweet potatoes and beans ahead of time.
